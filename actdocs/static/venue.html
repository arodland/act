The Venue for YAPC – NA 2011 is the Asheville Crowne Plaza Resort.

Thoughtfully designed to respect and celebrate our unique natural environment, the resort greets visitors with towering stone walls, rich hardwood accents, sumptuous furnishings and, during the cool season, the comforting warmth of a grand stone fireplace.
