Crowne Plaza Resort — Asheville, NC

How to Book

The Venue for YAPC – NA 2011 is the Asheville Crowne Plaza Resort.

Thoughtfully designed to respect and celebrate our unique natural environment, the resort greets visitors with towering stone walls, rich hardwood accents, sumptuous furnishings and, during the cool season, the comforting warmth of a grand stone fireplace.

    • How to Book

    The YAPC::NA Organizers have negotiated a discounted rate for staying *at* the hotel.

    Double $98.00
    King $109.00
    Villa - One Bedroom $139.00
    Villa - Two Bedroom $159.00

    The rooms each have two bed and can sleep up to four people, and the hotel is willing and able to split the bill for a room across multiple people. We are also prepared to assist people in finding roommates for those who are attending by themselves. Please see the Wiki for assistance.

    To book the discount rate you can call the hotel at either the toll-free number 1-800-733-3211 or local number 828-254-3211. You must mention that you are associated with "The Perl Foundation". Alternatively if you book online via the website. You can use the code "TPF".

    If would like to book a villa: Villas cannot be reserved on the website or at the 800 number. Attendees reserving villas must call our local number 828-254-3211 and ask for the “in-house” reservations office and mention that you are with "The Perl Foundation".
