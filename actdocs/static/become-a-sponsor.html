Become a Sponsor

Becoming a sponsor is very easy. Simply contact us with any questions or concerns you may have, and let us know what sponsorship level you might be interested in. If you're ready to sponsor now, scroll down and use any of the available sponsorship methods below.

Sponsor via Credit Card

To sponsor via credit card, use The Perl Foundation's donation system on donor.com:

  1. Go to https://secure.donor.com/pf012/give
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page for YAPC::NA.
  3. Type in the dollar amount for your sponsorship level.
  4. Click "add to cart"
  5. Complete the checkout process.
